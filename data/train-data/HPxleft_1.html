Happy!
By Felix Wolf on August 13, 2015
Verified Purchase
Beautiful all around. Very happy with my purchase. Great screen. Fast enough. Runs totally silent.
My 2 year old Dell AIO 2330 Inspiron had just died as a result of the Windows 10 upgrade. A graphics driver conflict generated a series of problems that not even a new motherboard could fix. Probably overheating destroyed the processor, so, to move on i decided to get this one.
Considering the somewhat plasticky feel, the moderate processor and graphics card, i would have given 4 stars only, but the price is so compelling that it deserves 5 stars. Just got it 2 days ago, so i have no idea how it will hold up. Will update this review if anything happens.
I upgraded the machine by exchanging the HDD with a 480 GB SSD i still had from the Dell. This, of course, made a big difference. It improved boot times from about 60 to 70 seconds from start to desktop to about 20 seconds, as measured by 'BootRacer'. Pure boot time is now 7 seconds.
I am also using using a Logitech bluetooth keyboard and mouse i already had. The wireless keyboard and mouse that come with the computer are un-exiting, especially the mouse is flimsy.
Upgrading to Windows 10 worked fine, except at one point there was a major issue with the Settings App not loading, which i was able to fix with a downloadable Microsoft troubleshooter, addressing 'Emerging Issue 67758' (searchable)

Pros:
Sleek, clean, modern design
Huge bright, beautiful screen with great colors, not too reflective
Totally silent, energy efficient
